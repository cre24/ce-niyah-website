Above is a video walkthrough of a system I designed for INLS 382. For the assignment, we were asked to identify an issue we experienced and to design a solution to the issue. An common issue I noticed my first year in college was the laundry room. There are many students in dorms that need to use the same washers and dryers. I designed a system that notifies students when a washer and dryer is available, notifies students when there clothes are finished washing and drying, and allows them to schedule a time to wash their clothes.
